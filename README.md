# campus-bloom-log-
A centralized student activity tracking platform to manage certifications, internships, hackathons, projects, workshops, and academic achievements in one secure digital portfolio.

✓PROJECT TITLE
Campus Bloom Log: A Centralized Digital Platform for Student Activity Records

✓PROBLEM STATEMENT
Students store certificates, internships, workshops, hackathons, projects, and achievements in different places, making them difficult to organize, verify, and share during placements or higher education applications.

✓SOLUTION
Campus Bloom Log provides one secure platform where students can upload, organize, manage, and share all their academic and extracurricular achievements.

✓OBJECTIVES
Maintain a digital student portfolio.
Store all achievements securely.
Enable easy sharing with recruiters and faculty.
Track student progress over time.
Reduce paperwork and lost certificates.

✓TARGET USERS
College Students
Faculty Members
Placement Cell
College Administration
Recruiters (through shared portfolio links)
